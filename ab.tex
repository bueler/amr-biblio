The literature of adaptive mesh refinement (AMR) for finite element (FE) methods is quite big, now in 2025, and I have missed too many citations in this space, even for the restricted literature of AMR for variational inequalities and obstacle problems.  In working on \cite{FochesattoBueler2025}, and the associated Python/Firedrake library  \href{https://github.com/StefanoFochesatto/viamr}{\texttt{viamr}}, we evidently need a place to keep track of, and provide context for, these references.

This is an annotated bibliography, and likely to be opinionated.
